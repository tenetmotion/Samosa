# Code of Conduct

Samosa contributors must communicate professionally and make participation safe and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, and publication of private information are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when conduct is harmful to the project or its community. Reports should be made privately through the repository's security-reporting or maintainer-contact channel. Maintainers will protect reporter privacy where practical and will explain enforcement decisions when appropriate.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the project.
